RAINBOW DRIVE-IN GRAVY
Simmer for 3 hours:
    1 qt stock
    Kombu, small size
    Dried shrimp
    Ginger, chunks
Make spice mix in bulk:
    5 t chili powder: 1 t each garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, and cumin
Strain then add:
    2 oz tomato paste
    2 T brown sugar
    2 t spice mix
    1 t minced garlic
    Worcestershire Sauce
    Salt and pepper
Simmer for 1 hour then thicken with:
    Flour Slurry
