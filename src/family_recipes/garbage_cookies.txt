GARBAGE COOKIES
Cream:
    1 stick butter
    1 c sugar
    1 c brown sugar
Add:
    2 eggs
    1 t vanilla
Sift:
    2 c flour
    ½ t baking powder
    1 t baking soda
    ½ t salt
Add:
    2 c oatmeal
    2 c rice crispies
    1 c chopped nuts
    1 c chocolate chips
Combine dry and wet ingredients. Make into balls and bake 350 degrees for 12-15 minutes or until brown.
