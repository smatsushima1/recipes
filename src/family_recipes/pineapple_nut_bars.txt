PINEAPPLE NUT BARS
Combine until crumbly:
    1 stick butter
    1 1/2 c flour
    1 T baking powder
    Salt
Cream:
    4 eggs
    2 c sugar
Then add:
    20 oz can pineapple chunks, drained then blended
    1 c chopped roasted nuts
Combine dry and wet ingredients. Bake at 350 degrees for 35 to 40 minutes. Dust top with powdered sugar.
