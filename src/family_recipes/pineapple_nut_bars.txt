PINEAPPLE NUT BARS
Combine until crumbly:    1 stick butter    1 1/2 c flour    1 T baking powder    SaltCream:    4 eggs    2 c sugarThen add:    20 oz can pineapple chunks, drained then blended    1 c chopped roasted nutsCombine dry and wet ingredients. Bake at 350 degrees for 35 to 40 minutes. Dust top with powdered sugar.
