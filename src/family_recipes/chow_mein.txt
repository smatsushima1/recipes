CHOW MEIN
Combine:
    2 lbs noodles
    1/4 c oyster sauce
    Sesame oil
Bake at 250 degrees for 10 min. Fry lightly:
    1 lb mushrooms, thinly sliced
    3/4 lb green beans, slivered
    4 stalks celery, thinly sliced
    1 pkg bean sprouts
    1/2 c green onions
    1/2 c Chinese peas
Combine with and toss lightly:
    3/4 lb char siu
    Noodles
    1/2 c oyster sauce
