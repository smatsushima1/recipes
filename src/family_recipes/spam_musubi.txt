SPAM MUSUBI
*Will take 2 hours to completePrepare:    5 c Japanese riceFry till golden brown both sides:    2 cans Spam, 8 slices each canDrain out all fat and add to pan till sugar dissolves:    1/2 c shoyu    1/2 c mirin, optional    1/4 c sugarAdd spam back into pan and simmer for 15 minutes uncovered. Form in mold, baste nori with leftover rehydrated sauce.
