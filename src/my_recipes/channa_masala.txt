CHANNA MASALA
Flash fry till cumin turns light brown:    4 T butter    1 t cuminAdd and flash fry:    2 T ginger    1 T garlicAdd and stir vigorously till tomato loses tinny smell:    2 T tomato pasteAdd and simmer uncovered till thickens:    4 c chickpeas    3 c water    1 T lemon juice    2 t curry powder    1 t cumin    1 t cayenne pepper, or to taste
