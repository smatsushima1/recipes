KIMCHI (MY VERSION)
Cut into chunks, combine and let rest:    1 head cabbage, around 2000 g    Salt, 2% of cabbage weightCombine and simmer for 10 minutes, place in another container,  then let sit till cool:    1 c water    1/4 c rice flour    1 T sugarJulienne:    2 c Korean radish    1 c Carrot    1 bunch green onionsOnce rice flour mix is cool, blend till smooth:    Rice flour mix    2 T garlic    1 T ginger    1 c onions    1 c Korean chili powder    1/4 c extra hot chili powderOnce cabbage is wilted, mix everything. Let sit in the refrigerator for at least 1 month.
