MAYONNAISE
Slowly whisk:
    4 egg yolks
Have oil in squeeze bottle. Gently squeeze at first then squeeze more when enough has been added:
    3 c light olive oil
Or, in food processor, pulse lightly:
    1 egg and 1 egg yolk
Have oil in squeeze bottle. Gently squeeze at first then squeeze more when enough has been added:
    3 c light olive oil, or until thickened
