MASHED POTATO SALAD
Simmer for 12 minutes or until tender, then put back on stove and turn off heat with lid on for 10 minutes:
    1200g potatoes, quartered
Mash, then add:
    Peas, cooked
    Carrots, shredded
    Onions, finely diced
    Eggs, hardboiled and diced
    1.5 c Mayonnaise
    Salt and pepper
