BEAN, POTATO, AND GREENS CURRY
Stir constantly for 10 minutes:    2 T ginger    4 T green chiles    1 T cumin    1 T chilli powder    1/2 T turmeric    1/2 T asafetida    OilAdd curry mix and simmer until beans tender:    3 c beans, soaked overnight    1 T garam masalaAdd and simmer until everything is tender:    1800 g potatoes, diced    3 c greens
