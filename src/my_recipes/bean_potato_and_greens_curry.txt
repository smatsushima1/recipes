BEAN, POTATO, AND GREENS CURRY
Stir constantly for 10 minutes:
    2 T ginger
    4 T green chiles
    1 T cumin
    1 T chilli powder
    1/2 T turmeric
    1/2 T asafetida
    Oil
Add curry mix and simmer until beans tender:
    3 c beans, soaked overnight
    1 T garam masala
Add and simmer until everything is tender:
    1800 g potatoes, diced
    3 c greens
