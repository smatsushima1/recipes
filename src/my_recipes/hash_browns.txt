HASH BROWNS
Grate in box grater:    2 potatoes, peeledFry in pan mixing frequently with generous oil, keep cover on for 5 minutes, add more oil, flip over, keep lid on till potatoes form into a solid mass that slides with pan shaking, around 3 minutes. Keep lid off for remainder of frying and re-flip both sides till crust is thick. Serves 2 people.
