MULTIGRAIN RICE
Soak overnight:    1 c barley, in cooking pan    1 c black rice, separate container    1 c red rice, with black riceDrain barley, then add, bring to boil, and simmer for 10 minutes:    4 c water (1:1 ratio of water:total cups of grain)    BarleyAdd with barley, bring to boil, and simmer for 20 minutes:    Black and red rice drained    1 c white rice, cleaned and drainedLet sit for at least 30 minutes.
