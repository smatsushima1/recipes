FOCACCIA
Heat for 45 seconds:    400g waterAdd, stir in, and let sit for 10 minutes:    2 1/4 t yeastMix in respective order:    1/2 c oil    600g flourKnead until it forms into a ball. Add oil to bowl and let rise for 1-2 hours. Spread onto oiled pan and let sit for 1 hour. Bake at 425 for 25 minutes or until golden brown.
